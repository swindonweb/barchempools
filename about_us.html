About Us

Barchem Pools is a family-run business founded in 1974.

Based in Corsham, Wiltshire, the business is currently run by Mark Self and although Barchem originally was more focused on pool construction, it now specialises in the maintenance and renovation of existing swimming pools.

This includes relining & supplying pool chemicals.

Our services also extend to the supply and installation of spas and hot tubs.

We can also provide advice and maintenance on pool buildings and enclosures, and, more recently, gaining a huge amount of experience and knowledge of sustainable heating alternatives such as air source pumps.

Mark and his team are always here to ask any questions you may have and we are more than happy to pay a visit to discuss your requirements at no obligation.
