Heat Pumps

Heat pumps are becoming very popular as a sustainable way to reduce the cost of heating swimming pools.

They, in effect, take heat from the outside air and use that to efficiently warm the water before it's heated to the proper temperature. A crucial few degrees that really saves money and energy in getting your swimming pool to the right temperature and keeping it there.

At Barchem, we've developed an extensive knowledge of air source heat pumps over the last 5 years and can advise you on all aspects of installation and on-going operation.
